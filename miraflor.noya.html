What is Nursing?

Nursing is a vital profession in healthcare that involves caring for individuals, families, and communities. Nurses support patient health and recovery through direct care, education, and emotional support.

What is Nursing Informatics?

Nursing Informatics combines nursing science with computer and information science. It helps nurses access accurate data, manage health records, and improve patient outcomes using technology.

How Technology Helps Nurses

  • Electronic Health Records (EHRs) reduce paperwork and errors
  • Real-time data improves patient monitoring and decision-making
  • Telehealth expands access to care
  • Mobile apps and AI tools help in medication safety and documentation

Roles in Nursing Informatics

  • Clinical Informatics Nurse Specialist
  • EHR Trainer or Superuser
  • Healthcare IT Project Manager
  • Quality Improvement Analyst
